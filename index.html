THE CONSULTANCY CANVAS

From Foundation to Accreditation

Founded in the United Kingdom, EduAdvance is a specialist consultancy dedicated exclusively to schools. We help institutions establish strong foundations and achieve internationally recognised accreditation — British, Canadian, American, Cognia, WASC and IB.

Step 01

Foundation & Feasibility

Market analysis, licensing guidance, governance planning and school concept development.

Step 02

Curriculum & Policy Design

Curriculum selection, academic frameworks, safeguarding, HR and operational policies.

Step 03

Recruitment & Leadership

Support with hiring, leadership structures, staff development and school culture.

Step 04

Accreditation Readiness

Preparation for CIS, COBIS, Cognia, WASC, IB, BSO and national inspection frameworks.
