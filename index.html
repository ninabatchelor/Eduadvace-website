Accreditation & Quality Assurance

Preparing Schools for International Recognition

Accreditation is more than an inspection—it is an opportunity to strengthen every aspect of school performance.

EduAdvance supports schools through every stage of the accreditation journey, from initial self-evaluation and strategic planning to documentation review, staff preparation and mock inspections.

Our experienced consultants work alongside school leaders to develop sustainable systems that continue to benefit schools long after the accreditation process has concluded.

✔ Self-Evaluation

Detailed school reviews against international quality standards.

✔ Strategic Planning

Clear action plans with measurable objectives and timelines.

✔ Documentation Review

Policies, procedures and evidence checked before accreditation visits.

✔ Staff Preparation

Workshops and coaching to ensure staff are confident and inspection ready.
