OUR TEAM

Leadership Experience That Makes a Difference

EduAdvance brings together a multidisciplinary team of 23 experienced former Heads of School, Executive Leaders, Principals, Curriculum Specialists, Accreditation Advisors, HR Consultants and specialist support professionals.

Every consultant has first-hand experience leading schools, managing change, improving teaching and learning, preparing for international accreditation and building sustainable school improvement strategies. Our advice is based on practical leadership experience rather than theory alone.

We understand the challenges faced by school owners, governing bodies and senior leadership teams because we have sat in those positions ourselves. From opening new schools and developing leadership capacity to achieving accreditation and improving educational outcomes, our team provides solutions that are realistic, strategic and designed for long-term success.

Working collaboratively across the United Kingdom, Canada, the United States, the Gulf States and Egypt, EduAdvance combines international expertise with local understanding, ensuring every school receives advice that is both globally informed and locally relevant.
